\subsection{Scattering from some solid objects} \label{sec:solidObject}

Figure~\ref{fig:solidObjects} shows results from the scattering of a Gaussian plane wave
from a collection of solid dispersive spheres.

See \texttt{cg/mx/runs/solidObjects}.
